# About Me

Hey there I'm Ryan and I was told only the cool kids use GitHub pages. Well I am here to prove them wrong! I came into the industry back in 2016 as a software engineer and have since pivoted to a role in DevOps. Both worlds (SWE and DevOps) come with a unique set of their own challenges and have an implicit demand on the workforce to stay current. 

This here blog is my attempt to document on whatever I feel I want to experiment with next. Anything spanning infrastructure as code, CI/CD, new languages or frameworks, and general development and devops philosophy are fair game.
